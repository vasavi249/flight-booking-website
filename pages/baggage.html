Baggage Information

Cabin Baggage

All passengers are entitled to bring one piece of hand luggage and one small personal item (such as a laptop bag or handbag) on board.

  • Maximum dimensions: 55 x 38 x 20 cm (22 x 15 x 8 inches)
  • Maximum weight: 7kg (15lbs) for Economy, 14kg (30lbs) for Business/First
  • Must fit in the overhead bin or under the seat in front of you

Check-in Baggage

Check-in baggage allowances vary depending on your travel class and route.

Travel Class Allowance Max Weight per bag
Economy 2 Pieces 23kg (50lbs)
Premium Economy 2 Pieces 23kg (50lbs)
Business 2 Pieces 32kg (70lbs)
First Class 3 Pieces 32kg (70lbs)

Restricted Items

For the safety of all passengers, the following items are strictly prohibited in both cabin and check-in baggage:

  • Flammable liquids and solids
  • Explosives and fireworks
  • Corrosive materials
  • Lithium batteries (loose)
  • Radioactive materials
  • Toxic or infectious substances
