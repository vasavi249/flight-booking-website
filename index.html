      Frequently Asked Questions

      How do I book a flight?

      Simply use our search form at the top of the page. Select your origin, destination, dates, and passengers, then click search. You can then choose your preferred flight, select your seats, and proceed to payment.

      Can I cancel my ticket?

      Yes, you can cancel your ticket from the "My Bookings" section. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on your fare class and how close to departure you cancel.

      How much baggage is allowed?

      Economy class includes 1 cabin bag (7kg) and 1 checked bag (23kg). Premium classes include additional allowances. Extra baggage can be purchased during booking.
